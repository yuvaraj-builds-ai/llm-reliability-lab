# NovaCart Order Cancellation Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is fictional and created for demonstration.

## Before shipment
Customers may request cancellation through the order page while status is “Processing.” Requests are not guaranteed because warehouse packing can begin immediately. A successfully cancelled authorization is released within 24 hours, though a bank may take 3–5 business days to remove it.

## After shipment
Orders marked “Shipped” cannot be cancelled. The customer may refuse delivery or use the normal returns process after receipt. Personalized items cannot be cancelled once production begins. NovaCart cannot reroute parcels to a different country.
## Partial, duplicate, and gift orders

For orders containing several items, cancellation may be requested for any item still Processing. Packed or shipped items remain active. A mistaken duplicate follows the same rules and is not guaranteed cancellation. Third-party marketplace products may enter fulfillment immediately; availability appears on the order page. Gift recipients cannot cancel because only purchasers control payment and order changes. Cancellation removes unposted loyalty points. A promotional code used on a cancelled order may expire and is not automatically restored.
## Timing examples

An order showing Processing may still be too far into warehouse work to stop, so the button creates a request rather than a guarantee. An order showing Shipped must follow delivery and return procedures even if tracking has not updated. If one item in a three-item order has shipped, only the other Processing items can potentially be cancelled. Bank hold release is not a completed refund and may appear differently on an account statement. Customers should keep cancellation confirmation for reference.
