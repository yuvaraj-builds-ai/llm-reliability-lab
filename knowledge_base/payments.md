# NovaCart Payment Methods Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is a fictional demonstration company.

## Accepted methods
NovaCart accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, supported debit cards, NovaCart gift cards, and PayPal. Cash, checks, bank transfers, cryptocurrency, and cash on delivery are not accepted. One gift card may be combined with one card.

## Authorization and security
Payment is authorized when an order is placed and captured when packing begins. A declined payment may be retried only by the customer. NovaCart never asks customers to email complete card numbers or passwords. Billing-address verification may be required, and suspicious orders can be cancelled after review.
## Currency, failed payments, and gift cards

Orders are charged in the currency displayed at checkout. Issuers may add conversion or foreign-transaction fees that NovaCart does not control or refund. If authorization succeeds but no order is created, the temporary hold disappears on the issuer's schedule. NovaCart gift cards do not expire and work only in the demonstration store. They cannot be redeemed for cash, reloaded, or replaced after transfer. Risk review may pause an order and request limited verification through the official account page.
## Payment examples and privacy

An order paid with one gift card may use one additional accepted card for the balance; two payment cards cannot be split across one order. A pending card amount is an authorization and can differ from a final captured charge when an item is cancelled before packing. Customers should enter payment details only on the checkout page. Support may request the last four card digits to locate a transaction but will never request the complete number, security code, account password, or a one-time verification code.
