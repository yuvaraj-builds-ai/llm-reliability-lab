# NovaCart Loyalty Points Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is a fictional company created for demonstration.

## Earning and redemption
Members earn one point per whole US dollar of eligible merchandise after discounts, excluding taxes, shipping, gift cards, and returned items. Points post 14 days after delivery. One hundred points equal one US dollar at checkout; at least 500 points are required to redeem.

## Expiration and returns
Points expire after 12 months without an eligible purchase. They cannot be sold, transferred, exchanged for cash, or applied after an order is placed. A return removes points earned on that item. If a refund makes a points balance negative, future earned points first offset that balance.
## Eligibility and redemption limits

Points belong to the account used at checkout and cannot be added from another account. Guest purchases do not earn points. Fractional dollars round down. Up to 20,000 points may be redeemed per order, and they cannot reduce the merchandise subtotal below one dollar. Taxes, shipping, customs charges, and gift cards cannot be paid with points. Cancelled orders restore redeemed points after cancellation completes. NovaCart may remove points created by error or fraud. Membership has no fee, and unused points have no cash value when an account closes.
## Examples and account status

A member buying 49.95 US dollars of eligible merchandise earns 49 points after the posting delay. Taxes and delivery charges do not increase that amount. Five hundred points provide five dollars at checkout because the minimum redemption threshold has been met. A member with 450 points cannot redeem them yet. Making an eligible purchase resets the inactivity period for all unexpired points. Logging in, contacting support, returning an item, or redeeming existing points does not count as an eligible purchase for expiration purposes.
