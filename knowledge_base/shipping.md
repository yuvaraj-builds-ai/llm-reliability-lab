# NovaCart Shipping Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is a fictional company created solely for demonstration.

## Processing and delivery
In-stock orders are processed within one business day. Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days after processing; express delivery takes 1–2 business days. Orders placed after 2 p.m. Gulf Standard Time begin processing the next business day. Customized items may require three additional business days.

## Tracking and weekends
A tracking link is emailed after the carrier scans the parcel. Tracking may take 12 hours to update. NovaCart processes orders Monday through Friday, excluding fictional public holidays. Weekend delivery is not guaranteed, even for express orders.

## Delays
Weather, address errors, carrier disruption, and peak demand can delay delivery. Estimates are not guarantees. Customers should contact support when tracking has not changed for three business days. NovaCart will investigate but does not issue automatic refunds solely because an estimate was missed.
## Addresses, split shipments, and missing parcels

Customers are responsible for checking the delivery address before submitting an order. Support may correct it only while the order remains in Processing status. Once a carrier accepts a parcel, changes depend on the carrier and are not guaranteed. NovaCart may split an order when products leave different fictional warehouses. Each parcel receives its own tracking link, and the customer is charged only the shipping amount shown at checkout.

A parcel marked delivered should first be checked around the property, with household members, and with the carrier. If it remains missing after 24 hours, the customer should contact NovaCart within seven calendar days. NovaCart may request delivery evidence and may replace or refund a verified missing shipment. A replacement depends on current stock and begins a new delivery estimate.
