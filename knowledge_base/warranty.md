# NovaCart Product Warranty Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is a fictional demonstration company.

## Coverage
NovaCart-branded electronics include a 12-month limited warranty from delivery. It covers manufacturing defects under normal household use. Approved claims may result in repair, a refurbished replacement, or store credit when neither is practical.

## Exclusions and claims
The warranty excludes accidental damage, misuse, unauthorized repair, normal battery wear, cosmetic scratches, and lost products. Proof of purchase and the serial number are required. Customers should contact support before shipping a product; unauthorized shipping costs are not reimbursed. Third-party products use the manufacturer warranty stated on their product page.
## Review and replacement terms

Support may request photos, video, diagnostic results, and software version. Customers must back up and erase personal data before shipping; NovaCart is not responsible for lost data. Claim authorization lasts 15 calendar days. A replacement does not restart the original warranty and is covered for the longer of 90 days or the original period remaining. Color or packaging may differ when the original is unavailable, but core specifications will be equivalent. Service is limited to supported delivery regions and does not compensate for lost work, data, or third-party equipment.
## Practical boundaries

A laptop that stops charging under normal use may qualify after diagnosis, while a cracked screen after a fall does not. Battery capacity that gradually declines through ordinary use is normal wear, but a battery that fails because of a verified manufacturing defect may qualify. The warranty is separate from the shorter return window: an eligible defect can be reviewed after returns close. Customers should not hire an unauthorized repair shop before review because resulting damage and altered parts are excluded.
