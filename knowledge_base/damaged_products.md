# NovaCart Damaged Products Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is fictional and this policy is for demonstration.

## Reporting damage
Visible shipping damage or a wrong item must be reported within 48 hours of delivery. Customers should keep the packaging and provide the order number, photos of the item, label, and box. Hidden defects discovered later may be handled under the warranty.

## Resolution
NovaCart may provide replacement parts, a replacement item, or a full refund after review. NovaCart supplies a prepaid label when return is required. Original standard shipping is refunded for verified shipping damage or fulfillment error. Customers should not discard a damaged product until support confirms it is unnecessary.
## Inspection and replacement

Support may arrange carrier inspection or request more images. Customers must retain the parcel and packaging when inspection is scheduled. Damage caused after delivery, including drops or water exposure, is not shipping damage. An approved replacement ships after the return carrier scans the damaged item unless support waives return. Timing depends on stock and standard delivery is free. If unavailable, NovaCart may refund rather than substitute a different model. The 48-hour report window covers shipping damage; later manufacturing defects may still use the warranty process.
## Examples and boundaries

A crushed box and cracked product visible on delivery should use the 48-hour damage process. A product that arrives intact and is dropped the next week does not qualify as shipping damage. Receiving a different color or model from the order is treated as a fulfillment error under this policy. Photos should be clear enough to identify the product, damage, shipping label, and condition of the outer box. Filing a report does not require the customer to decide whether replacement or refund is best before review.
