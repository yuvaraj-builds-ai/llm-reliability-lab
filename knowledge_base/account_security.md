# NovaCart Account Security Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is a fictional demonstration company.

## Protecting an account
Customers should use a unique password and enable optional two-step verification. NovaCart staff never request a password, verification code, or complete payment-card number. Password-reset links expire after 30 minutes.

## Unknown or Unauthorized Orders and Suspected Compromise
Customers noticing unknown orders should reset their password, sign out other sessions, and contact support immediately. NovaCart may temporarily lock an account during review. Customers must contact their card issuer about unauthorized card charges. NovaCart does not guarantee recovery of loyalty points transferred before a report.
## Safe communication and account changes

Official messages direct customers to sign in through the demonstration site and never contain executable attachments. Unexpected links requesting payment or verification codes should not be followed. Changing an email requires password confirmation and verification at the new address. Two-step recovery may require saved recovery codes. During security review, refunds, address changes, and point redemption may be temporarily unavailable. Support can restore access but cannot see a password. Closing an account does not automatically cancel open orders or erase records needed for returns and refunds.
## Security examples

A message asking for a password or verification code is not a legitimate NovaCart support request. A customer who reused a compromised password should replace it with a unique password before checking orders and saved addresses. If an unknown order is still Processing, the customer may separately request cancellation, but account review does not guarantee the warehouse can stop it. Security reports should include the time and nature of suspicious activity without including secret credentials. Customers remain responsible for securing their email account and devices.
