# NovaCart Refund Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is a fictional demonstration company.

## Timing and method
NovaCart inspects returned items within three business days of receipt. Approved refunds are sent to the original payment method within five business days. Banks and card issuers may need another 2–7 business days to display the credit. Gift-card purchases are refunded to a NovaCart gift card.

## Fees and deductions
Original standard shipping is refunded only when NovaCart sent the wrong item or the product arrived damaged. Express upgrades are not refunded. A partial refund may apply when eligible goods have cosmetic wear, missing packaging, or missing nonessential accessories. The deduction is explained before processing.

## Rejected returns
Items outside the return window or with misuse may be sent back to the customer. NovaCart does not issue cash refunds for card purchases.
## Split payments and refund tracking

For payment made with a gift card and card, the gift-card portion is restored first and the remainder returns to the card. Discounts across several products are refunded proportionally. The confirmation email is sent when NovaCart submits a credit and contains a reference customers can give their bank. NovaCart cannot accelerate bank posting. If no credit appears after both processing periods, support can verify the payment method and provide a transaction reference. A chargeback may pause internal refund review until the bank process ends.
## Examples and corrections

A card refund approved on Monday is normally submitted by the following Monday when no fictional holiday intervenes; the issuer can then take up to seven additional business days. This is not a promise that every credit appears in five days. Refund timing begins after inspection and approval, not when the customer first ships the return. Where NovaCart refunds original standard shipping because of its own error, an optional express upgrade remains excluded. Customers receive an explanation for any condition deduction.
