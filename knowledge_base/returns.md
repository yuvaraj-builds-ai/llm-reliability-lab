# NovaCart Returns Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is fictional and this policy exists only for demonstration.

## Return windows
Most unused products may be returned within 30 calendar days of delivery. Opened laptops, tablets, and phones have a 14-day window. Opened electronics must be undamaged, reset, and include every accessory, manual, and original package.

## Conditions and proof
A NovaCart order number or gift receipt is required. Products with missing parts, misuse, liquid damage, or altered serial numbers are not eligible. Clothing may be tried on but must be unworn, unwashed, and tagged.

## Exclusions and process
Digital downloads, personalized goods, hygiene products with broken seals, and final-sale items are non-returnable unless defective. Customers must request a return authorization before sending an item. Domestic prepaid labels are provided for eligible returns; original shipping charges are handled under the refund policy.
## Authorization, exchanges, and gifts

An authorization is valid for ten calendar days. Customers should package products securely and erase personal data before shipment. NovaCart is not responsible for data left on devices. Products from different authorizations should not share a box. NovaCart does not perform direct exchanges; customers return an eligible item and place a separate order. Gift recipients with a gift receipt receive store credit. Promotional bundles must be returned together unless support confirms one item is defective. If a free promotional item is kept, its listed value may be deducted from the refund.
## Examples and boundaries

A sealed headset delivered on March 1 may normally be requested for return through March 31. An opened laptop delivered the same day must be requested by March 15 and meet the electronics conditions. Starting a request within the window does not make an excluded item eligible. Return shipping damage caused by weak customer packaging may reduce a refund. Support may ask for photographs before authorization when condition is unclear. These examples explain the written rules and do not extend any return window.
