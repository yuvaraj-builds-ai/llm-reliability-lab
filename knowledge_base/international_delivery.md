# NovaCart International Delivery Policy
Effective date: January 15, 2026. NovaCart is fictional and created solely for demonstration.

## Regions and timing
NovaCart ships internationally to Canada, the United Kingdom, European Union member states, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, Japan, and the United Arab Emirates. Delivery normally takes 7–14 business days after processing. Express international delivery is not offered.

## Duties and restrictions
Customers are responsible for import duties, taxes, and customs brokerage charges. Customs delays are outside NovaCart’s estimates. Batteries sold separately, aerosols, oversized furniture, and products restricted by destination law cannot be shipped internationally.

## International returns
Eligible international returns follow the product’s normal return window, but the customer pays return shipping unless the item was wrong or arrived damaged. International returns are therefore not automatically free. Duties paid to local authorities are not refunded by NovaCart.
## Customs information and exceptions

Customers must provide a complete local address, reachable phone number, and identification required by customs. NovaCart will not understate value or mark a purchase as a gift. If duties are declined and a parcel returns, the carrier return charge may be deducted from the merchandise refund. Remote areas may take longer and tracking can pause during customs review. If a destination becomes unavailable after ordering, NovaCart cancels and refunds the shipment. Customers should verify local plug type, voltage, and compatibility before purchase.
## Returns and refunds example

A customer in Canada returning an eligible item because they changed their mind pays tracked shipping back to NovaCart. If NovaCart sent the wrong item, NovaCart instead provides return instructions and covers reasonable return shipping. Merchandise refunds follow the refund policy after inspection, while customs tax recovery must be requested from the relevant local authority. An estimated 7–14 business-day delivery excludes processing and customs holds. Customers should not order restricted goods using a freight-forwarding address to bypass destination rules.
